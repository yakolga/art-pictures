Frequently Asked Questions

We will answer any of your questions

How long does it take to produce an order?

Order completion time depends on three factors: the painting's complexity, style, and size. The average order completion time is 7 days, excluding shipping. In some cases, at the customer's request, the deadline can be reduced to 5 days.

Note: If you decide to change something in the painting or make adjustments, this may take additional time, which is not included in the agreed completion time.

Will I be able to see the painting before I receive it?

Yes. Once completed, we'll email you a digital photo of your painting so you can make any adjustments or suggestions you may have. You can also see the painting in person at our showroom. Please let your manager know.

What format should the photos be?

We work with all popular image formats! You can upload JPEG, TIFF, and PNG files up to 50 megabytes in size through our website. You must own the copyright or have permission to use the image. If you have a different image format (RAW, PDF), you can use our FTP uploader. If you want to make a photo from a paper photograph (not digital), send it to us, we will scan, process and return the photo along with your order!

Can I give special instructions to the artist?

When you place an order on the website, you can add your comments in a special text field. You can also combine objects from different photos into one or remove objects from a photo, change the background, change clothes or outfits, create a color painting from a black-and-white photo, or order a painting from a damaged or old photo.
